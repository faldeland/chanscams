About Chans Cams

An owner-operated rental house for the people who actually shoot.

We started Chans Cams because indie productions deserve more than picked-over inventory and rigid policies. Every camera, lens, and battery in our locker gets prepped by the same people who answer your calls — so you know exactly what you're getting when the kit lands on set.

  • Tested, not assumed. Each rig is bench-checked before every booking.
  • Honest weekly rates. Five days for the price of four. Always.
  • Real backup. If a card fails or a cable dies on set, we'll get you a replacement.
  • Indie-friendly insurance. We accept short-term policies and one-off COIs.
